About us

Founded in 2018, Tamar Software has quickly established itself as a leader in the IT industry, specializing in cutting-edge software solutions and consultancy services. In 2024, the company transitioned to a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), marking a new chapter of growth and collaboration. Under the visionary leadership of CEO Pradeep Kumar Tamar, the company has expanded its expertise and offerings, becoming a trusted partner for businesses worldwide. Our emphasis on cutting-edge technology and client satisfaction positions us as a trusted partner for all IT needs.
